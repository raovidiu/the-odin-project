Simple & Zesty Homemade Guacamole

The secret to great guacamole isn't a long list of ingredients; it's the quality of the avocados and the balance of acid and salt. This recipe is chunky, fresh, and stays bright green thanks to a generous squeeze of lime juice. It's the perfect dip for chips or a creamy topper for your favorite tacos.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe Hass avocados
  • 1/2 small red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional for heat)
  • 1-2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse salt
  • A pinch of freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a medium bowl.
  2. Using a fork or potato masher, gently mash the avocado to your desired consistency (some like it smooth, others prefer it chunky).
  3. Stir in the diced onion, cilantro, jalapeño, and lime juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Taste and add more lime or salt if needed.
  5. Serve immediately or place plastic wrap directly on the surface of the guacamole to prevent browning.
