The Ultimate World-Famous Banana Bread

This isn't just any banana bread; it’s a moist, golden-brown masterpiece that perfectly balances sweetness with a hint of warm cinnamon. Whether you have overripe bananas sitting on your counter or you're just looking for the ultimate comfort food, this recipe delivers a tender crumb and a rich aroma that will fill your entire home. It’s simple to make, impossible to resist, and even better when served warm with a thick smear of salted butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 very ripe bananas, peeled
  • 1/3 cup melted butter, unsalted or salted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar (1/2 cup if you want it less sweet, 1 cup for more)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C), and butter a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour last.
  4. Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then remove the banana bread from the pan and let it cool completely before slicing.
