Classic Italiak Caprese Salad

The Caprese salad is a simple Italian masterpiece that celebrates the colors of the Italian flag: red, white, and green. It relies on the absolute freshness of its ingredients rather than complex cooking techniques. Perfect as a light appetizer or a side dish, this salad is a refreshing staple of the Neapolitan region.

Ingredients

  • 2 large, ripe vine tomatoes
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese (preferably Buffalo Mozzarella)
  • 1 bunch of fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil for drizzling
  • Balsamic glaze or reduction
  • Flaky sea salt and freshly cracked black pepper

Stepts to Prepare

  1. Slice the tomatoes and the mozzarella into 1/4-inch thick rounds.
  2. On a serving platter, alternate layers of tomato and mozzarella slices, overlapping them slightly.
  3. Tuck a fresh basil leaf between each layer of tomato and cheese.
  4. Drizzle generously with extra-virgin olive oil and a few drops of balsamic glaze.
  5. Season with sea salt and black pepper to taste. Serve immediately at room temperature.
